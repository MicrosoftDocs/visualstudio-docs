## How do I cancel a Visual Studio subscription purchased via the Retail Channel (Microsoft Store)?

To cancel a Visual Studio subscription purchased through the Retail Channel (Microsoft Store), start by signing in to your Microsoft account portal.

To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

1. To access your account settings, go to https://account.microsoft.com.
0. To identify the subscription you purchased, sign in with the Microsoft account used for your Visual Studio retail subscription.
0. To view your active products, select Services & subscriptions at the top.
0. To find the subscription you want to cancel, locate your Visual Studio subscription in the list.
0. To open its management options, select Manage.
0. To begin the cancellation process, choose Cancel subscription.

0. To confirm the cancellation, look for a confirmation email.

You might also visit Microsoft's account support at [Microsoft Store and Billing Support](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-store-and-billing).
